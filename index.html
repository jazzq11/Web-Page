About Me

Hello! I am Muhammad Jazib Qamar, a researcher affiliated with the ANT Lab (Advanced Forward Network Technology Lab) at CCU, Milan, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Jen Yi Pan. I have completed my BS in Electrical Engineering from PIEAS in 2024 and am currently focused on research in Wireless Communication, Next Generation Wireless Networks, Deep Learning, IoT, and Cyber-Physical Systems.

Background

I completed my BS in Electrical Engineering from PIEAS in 2024. Currently, I am conducting my research work under the supervision of Prof. Pan at the Advanced Networking Lab, CCU, Milan, Italy.

My research journey began under the supervision of Prof. Hafsa Iqbal at the Robotics and Machine Intelligence Lab at SEECS NUST. I am currently involved in collaborative research with the University of Cork, Ireland, the University of Brandon, Canada, Stanford University, USA, and PIEAS.
